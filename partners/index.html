Withdrawals

Clients can withdraw their funds at any time. PROFIT PAIRS FX LIMITED processes withdrawals on the same day. However, withdrawals processed on the same day are subject to clearance from the payment processor or bank clearing times.

Broker to Broker Transfer

If you are looking to transfer funds to PROFIT PAIRS FX LIMITED or from PROFIT PAIRS FX LIMITED to another broker, our client area allows you to do so.
Log in and complete the Broker to Broker form.

Leverage Change

Leverage can be a useful tool for taking maximum advantage of trading opportunities. However, increased leverage can also bring increased risk. Leverage changes are subject to review and must comply with SCB regulation.

Deposits

PROFIT PAIRS FX LIMITED keeps all the funds from clients in segregated accounts. This is to ensure that client funds are held separate from the business, giving clients the greatest level of security & maximum benefits for their funds. However, clients must deposit funds to their trading account from an account in their own name. 3rd party deposits are not accepted.
